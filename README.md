# 🌾 KrishiMitra AI

### AI-Powered Crop Disease Detection & Sustainable Farming Advisory for Indian Farmers

---

## 📖 About the Project

KrishiMitra AI is an AI-powered web application designed to help Indian farmers detect crop diseases at an early stage using smartphone images.

The application not only identifies crop diseases but also provides confidence score, disease severity, disease stage, Marathi advisory, organic treatment recommendations, and preventive measures.

Our goal is to reduce crop loss, minimize unnecessary pesticide usage, promote sustainable farming practices, and empower farmers with easy-to-understand agricultural guidance.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many Indian farmers are unable to identify crop diseases at an early stage.

Because of delayed detection,

- Crops suffer severe damage.
- Farmers use excessive pesticides.
- Farming costs increase.
- Crop productivity decreases.
- Soil health is affected.
- Farmers often lack access to agricultural experts.

Language barriers and limited internet connectivity make the situation even more difficult in rural areas.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

KrishiMitra AI allows farmers to simply capture a photo of an affected crop leaf.

Our AI system analyzes the image and instantly provides:

- 🌿 Disease Detection
- 📊 Confidence Score
- 📈 Disease Severity Score
- 🧭 Disease Stage
- 📖 Disease Information
- 🗣️ Marathi Advisory
- 🌱 Organic Treatment Recommendation
- 🛡️ Safe Chemical Recommendation (only if required)
- 📋 Prevention Tips

This enables farmers to make timely and informed decisions.

## 🗣️ Marathi Advisory

## 🌱 Organic Treatment

Suggests eco-friendly treatment methods before chemical usage.
