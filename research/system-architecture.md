# System Architecture

## Overview

KrishiMitra AI is an AI-powered sustainable farming assistant that helps farmers detect crop diseases, understand disease severity, and receive sustainable treatment recommendations in Marathi.

---

## System Flow

Farmer

↓

Capture Crop Image

↓

Frontend (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)

↓

Flask Backend (Python)

↓

Image Preprocessing (OpenCV)

↓

AI Model (MobileNetV2)

↓

Disease Prediction

↓

Recommendation Engine

↓

Generate Results

- Disease Name
- Confidence Score
- Disease Severity Score
- Disease Stage
- Disease Information
- Marathi Advisory
- Organic Treatment
- Safe Chemical Treatment (if required)
- Prevention Tips

↓

Display Results to Farmer
