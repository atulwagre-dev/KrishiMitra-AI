
# 🌾 KrishiMitra AI - Features

## Overview

KrishiMitra AI is an intelligent farming assistant designed to help farmers identify crop diseases, receive personalized recommendations, and improve agricultural productivity. The platform supports multiple Indian languages and works even in low-connectivity rural areas.

---

# Core Features

## 🌐 Multi-Language Support

- Marathi
- Hindi
- English
- User-selected language is applied across the application.
- AI recommendations are displayed in the selected language.

---

## 🌱 Crop Selection

- Select crop before disease analysis.
- Supports multiple crops.
- Crop information stored for AI prediction.

Supported crops include:

- Tomato
- Potato
- Rice
- Wheat
- Cotton
- Soybean

---

## 📷 Disease Detection

- Upload crop leaf image.
- AI detects disease.
- Displays disease confidence score.
- Displays disease severity.
- Displays disease stage.

---

## 🤖 AI Recommendation

Provides:

- Disease Name
- Disease Description
- Confidence Score
- Severity Level
- Disease Stage
- Recommended Treatment
- Organic Treatment
- Chemical Treatment (if required)
- Prevention Tips

---

## 🌡 Temperature Monitoring

- Displays crop-friendly temperature.
- Helps farmers monitor environmental conditions.

---

## 🌾 Soil Check

- Soil health guidance.
- Basic soil recommendations.
- Future IoT sensor integration.

---

## 🧮 Fertilizer Calculator

- Calculates fertilizer requirement.
- Crop-wise fertilizer recommendations.
- Simple fertilizer planning.

---

## 📅 Fertilizer Scheduler

- Reminder for fertilizer application.
- Crop-wise schedule.
- Notification-ready architecture.

---

## 🌦 Weather Alerts

- Weather forecast.
- Rain alerts.
- Temperature alerts.
- Crop protection guidance.

---

## 📍 Krishi Kendra Locator

- Nearby Krishi Kendra information.
- Agricultural support center details.
- Government assistance access.

---

## 📴 Offline AI

- Works with offline AI model.
- Suitable for rural areas with poor internet.
- Local inference support.

---

## 💬 Feedback System

- Farmers can submit feedback.
- Helps improve recommendations.
- User satisfaction tracking.

---

## ❓ Help & AI Assistant

- AI-powered farming assistant.
- Answers farming questions.
- Supports multilingual conversations.
