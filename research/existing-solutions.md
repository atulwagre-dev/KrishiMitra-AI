# Existing Solutions Analysis

## Purpose

Before building KrishiMitra AI, we studied existing agriculture applications to understand their strengths, limitations, and identify opportunities for innovation.

---

## 1. Plantix

### Features
- AI-based crop disease detection
- Pest identification
- Fertilizer recommendations
- Community support

### Limitations
- Requires internet for many features
- Limited offline functionality
- No dedicated Marathi offline advisory
- Limited focus on sustainable treatment recommendations

---

## 2. AgriCentral

### Features
- Weather forecast
- Crop advisory
- Market prices
- Agriculture news

### Limitations
- No AI disease detection
- No image-based diagnosis
- Focuses mainly on information services

---

## 3. Kisan Suvidha

### Features
- Government schemes
- Weather information
- Market prices

### Limitations
- No Computer Vision
- No AI disease detection
- No personalized recommendations

---

## 4. PlantVillage Nuru

### Features
- AI disease detection
- Offline detection support

### Limitations
- Supports limited crops
- Limited regional language support
- Limited sustainability recommendations

---

# Why KrishiMitra AI?

KrishiMitra AI combines:

- AI Crop Disease Detection
- Offline-first Design
- Marathi Advisory
- Sustainable Farming Recommendations
- Future IoT Integration
