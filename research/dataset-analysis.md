# Dataset Analysis

## Selected Dataset

**PlantVillage Dataset**

## Why We Selected This Dataset

We selected the PlantVillage dataset because it is one of the most widely used public datasets for crop disease detection. It contains high-quality labeled images and is suitable for training AI models for plant disease classification.

## Dataset Details

- Dataset Name: PlantVillage
- Total Images: ~54,000+
- Crops Covered: 14+
- Disease Classes: 38
- Image Type: RGB Leaf Images
- License: Research & Educational Use

## Advantages

- High-quality labeled images
- Publicly available
- Widely used in AI research
- Suitable for deep learning models
- Good benchmark dataset

## Limitations

- Images are captured under controlled conditions.
- Real farm conditions may have different lighting and backgrounds.
- Additional field images may be required for better real-world accuracy.

## Future Improvement

- Fine-tune the model using real farm images collected from Indian farms.
- Support more Indian crops.
- Improve prediction under varying weather and lighting conditions.

## Why PlantVillage?

PlantVillage provides a reliable starting point for building an AI-powered crop disease detection system. It enables rapid prototype development while maintaining good prediction accuracy.
