# Project Scope

## Included in Hackathon MVP

- Crop image upload
- AI disease detection
- Disease information
- Organic treatment recommendations
- Prevention tips
- Marathi advisory


## Future Scope

- 📍 **Nearest Krishi Kendra Locator** – Find nearby agriculture service centers using GPS.

- 📶 **Offline AI Prediction** – Enable disease detection without internet connectivity.

- 🌦️ **Weather-Based Disease Alerts** – Predict disease risk using weather forecasts.

- 🌱 **IoT Smart Farming Integration** – Connect soil moisture, temperature, and humidity sensors for real-time crop monitoring.

- 🎙️ **Voice Assistant (Marathi)** – Allow farmers to interact with the app using voice commands.

- 🧑‍🌾 **Government Schemes & Subsidy Information** – Provide personalized government scheme recommendations.

- 📈 **Crop Health History Dashboard** – Track disease history and crop health over time.

- 🛰️ **Drone & Satellite Monitoring** – Monitor large farms using aerial imagery for early disease detection.

- 🤖 **AI Yield Prediction** – Predict crop yield based on disease status, weather, and farm conditions.

- 📊 **Farmer Analytics Dashboard** – Provide insights on disease trends and treatment effectiveness.
